Terms and privacy for AI try-on.

This page explains how AIWardrobe handles subscriptions, credits, uploaded photos, generated images, and account data.

Read privacy policy Read terms

Terms of service

By creating an account or using AIWardrobe, you agree to use the service responsibly and to follow the subscription, credit, and content rules below.

Account

You are responsible for the accuracy of your account information and for keeping access to your account secure.

Subscriptions and credits

One generated image costs one credit. Paid packages are processed through the app store billing provider. Subscriptions renew unless they are canceled before renewal.

Uploaded content

You must only upload photos and outfit images you have the right to use. Do not upload illegal, abusive, explicit, or harmful content.

Generated results

AI output can vary. AIWardrobe does not guarantee that every generated image will be accurate, realistic, or suitable for every use case.

Privacy policy

AIWardrobe collects the minimum data needed to provide virtual try-on, account access, subscription handling, and support.

Data we process

  • Account details such as email, name, and login records.
  • Uploaded photos, outfit images, and generated results.
  • Credit balance, generation history, and payment references.
  • Support messages and basic device or usage diagnostics.

How we use data

We use data to generate try-on images, maintain saved photos, manage credits and subscriptions, prevent abuse, improve reliability, and respond to support requests.

Service providers

Uploaded photos and outfit images may be sent to configured AI processing providers. Payment processing is handled by app store and subscription providers; AIWardrobe does not store raw card data.

Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active or as required for operational and legal reasons. You may request account deletion, which removes personal data, photos, and generation history from active systems subject to required retention duties.

AIWardrobe does not sell personal data to advertisers. User photos are used to provide the try-on experience, maintain reliability, prevent abuse, and support account features.

Questions or data requests

For access, correction, deletion, subscription, or privacy requests, contact AIWardrobe support.
